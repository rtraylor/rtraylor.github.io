Here are some of our current projects and interests

Implementation of Stochastic Optimization Algorithms

Participants: C. Traylor

Implementing various stochastic optimization algorithms for Markov decision processes for use in manufacturing and retail establishments

Disintegration of Measures and Structural Dependence

Participants: J. Hathcock, C. Traylor, M. Kukla

This project is more theoretical in nature. We are trying to adapt our structural dependence probability models into a more general measure-theoretic framework.

Fuzzy Time Series

Participants: C. Traylor and M. Kukla

We propose a new type of time series modeling, in which we aggregate high granularity data sets into fuzzy numbers, thus avoiding the typical loss of information when aggregation is performed.

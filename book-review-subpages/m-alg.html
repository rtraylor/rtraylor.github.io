Algebra

  • A Book of Abstract Algebra C. Pinter
    One of the friendliest introductions to abstract algebra you'll find.
  • Algebra T. Hungerford
    A dense dictionary of abstract algebra that's better as a reference than a textbook
  • Kronecker Products and Matrix Calculus with Applications A. Graham
    A reader will need a good background in matrix theory before using this book. Contains matrix calculus generally not found elsewhere, but not a particularly well-written text.
  • Linear Algebra Done Right S. Axler
    Pretentious title with an underwhelming review.
